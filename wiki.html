Is the server on offline mode? Can I join with cracked?


Yes you can join from cracked accounts. However keep it in mind that we do not support piracy!

Which version is the server on?


The server version is 1.18.2 (Java) and 1.18.30 (Bedrock) However you can join from older / newer versions too!
We recommend using 1.18.x for full experience
However if your PC/Mobile can not handle it , you can use 1.17.x
BUT even if that is very laggy for you , you can use the versions 1.16.x (Java only)
NOTE - In versions 1.16.x you will not get the full experience
You can not go below y0 and above y256 and you will miss out on MANY features!

What are YouTube / Streamer rank requirements?


For Youtubers ->
- Minimum 300 subscribers!
- At least 2 videos on the server with 200+ views before applying!
- Must post at least 2 videos on the server in a month!
- The videos must be over 5 mins!
- The videos must be public and not unlisted / private!

For Streamers ->
- Minimum 300 followers!
- At least 1 stream on the server with 200+ views before applying!
- The stream must be over 1 hour!
- Must stream at least twice a month!
- The stream must be public and not unlisted / private!

How do I link my account? Why should I link my account?


You need to DM SlimeCraft Utility#2152 the 4 digit code you get after running the command `/discordsrv link` to link your Minecraft account to your Discord account.
You need to link your Minecraft account to your Discord account for the proper functioning of the proximity VC and many other features.

How do I register? How do I login? Why do I have to register and login?


To register you have to do /register (password) (password).
To login you have to do /login (password).
Example
- After linking my account and joining the server I do /register PhoenixIsCool PhoenixIsCool
- The next time I join I don't have to register again BUT do /login PhoenixIsCool

Note - Keep a strong password because if someone knows your password he/she can login to the server through your account
Another Note - Please remember your password
You have to register and login for the security of your own account otherwise people can use your account and waste hours of your grind in seconds.
