Support

If you have any issues with the server, please contact us through our Discord Server by creating a ticket.
HOW TO CREATE TICKET:
Step 1: Verify your discord account in game by following the steps in the discord server.
Step 2: Go to #ticket-here

Step 3: Read the rules and follow the Steps given in the channel

Step 4: Once you have created a ticket, describe the issue, the staff will contact you as soon as possible.

OR you can contact us by filling a google form.
