Support

If you have any issues with the server, please contact us through the form. We will get back to you as soon as possible. Or you can contact us through our discord server by creating a ticket. Click here to join our discord server
HOW TO CREATE TICKET:
Step1: Verify your discord account in game by following the steps in the discord server.
Step2: Go to #ticket-here and follow the steps given there.
Step3: Once you have created a ticket, describe the issue, the staff will contact you shortly.
